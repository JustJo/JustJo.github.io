~ SNAKE ~

Use the up/down/left/right arrows on your keyboard to move the snake. Game ends when snake runs into a wall, or runs into itself.

Use the up/down/left/right buttons to move the snake. Game ends when snake runs into a wall, or runs into itself.

Game Key:

Food
Eat this to grow snake by 1 square, score increases by 10 points.

Accelerate
Eat this to grow snake by 1 square and double snake speed for 15 seconds. During that time, points are doubled.

Reverse
Eat this to grow snake by 1 square and to reverse your controls (up goes down, left goes right, etc). Reversal ends once another reversal is eaten. During reversal, points are doubled.

Poison
Eat this and your snake length will decrease by 1 square, score decreases by 10 points
